Basic English Grammar

Pelajari dasar-dasar tata bahasa Inggris dengan mudah dan menyenangkan. Artikel ini akan membantu Anda memahami struktur kalimat dasar dalam bahasa Inggris.

Daftar Isi
  • 1. Subject dan Predicate
  • 2. Simple Present Tense
  • 3. Personal Pronouns
  • 4. Latihan

1. Subject dan Predicate

Setiap kalimat dalam bahasa Inggris memiliki dua bagian utama:

Subject (Subjek)

Pelaku dalam kalimat (who/what)

  • I, You, He, She, It, We, They
  • John, Mary, The cat, My friend
Predicate (Predikat)

Apa yang dilakukan oleh subject

  • eat, sleep, study, write
  • is reading, was running, will go

2. Simple Present Tense

Simple Present Tense digunakan untuk:

Kegiatan sehari-hari:
  • I eat breakfast every morning.
  • She studies English twice a week.
  • They play football on Sunday.

3. Personal Pronouns

Subject Object Possessive
I me my/mine
You you your/yours
He him his

4. Latihan

Lengkapi kalimat berikut:
  1. _____ (I/Me) study English.
  2. This is _____ (my/mine) book.
  3. _____ (He/Him) plays football.

Jawaban:

  1. I study English.
  2. This is my book.
  3. He plays football.
