1. Greetings (Salam)

Berikut adalah beberapa salam umum dalam bahasa Inggris:

Person A: Good morning!

Person B: Good morning! How are you?

Person A: I'm fine, thank you. And you?

Person B: I'm good too, thanks!

Penjelasan:
  • Good morning = Selamat pagi
  • How are you? = Apa kabar?
  • I'm fine = Saya baik-baik saja

2. Introduction (Perkenalan)

Sarah: Hi! I'm Sarah. What's your name?

John: Hello Sarah, I'm John. Nice to meet you!

Sarah: Nice to meet you too! Where are you from?

John: I'm from Canada. How about you?

Kosa Kata Penting:
  • What's your name? = Siapa namamu?
  • Nice to meet you = Senang bertemu denganmu
  • Where are you from? = Dari mana asalmu?

3. At the Restaurant

Waiter: Good evening! May I take your order?

Customer: Yes, I'd like a chicken sandwich, please.

Waiter: Would you like anything to drink?

Customer: Just water, thank you.

Ungkapan Penting:
  • May I take your order? = Boleh saya catat pesanan Anda?
  • I'd like... = Saya ingin...
  • Would you like...? = Apakah Anda ingin...?

4. Practice Time

Latihan Dialog

Lengkapi dialog berikut:

Person A: Good afternoon! _______ (how/what) is your name?

Person B: Hi! My name is _______.

Person A: _______ to meet you!

Person B: Nice to meet you _______!

Jawaban:

1. what
2. [your name]
3. Nice
4. too
